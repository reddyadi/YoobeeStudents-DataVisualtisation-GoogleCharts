Distance Students Travel to get to Yoobee


This Scatter Plot shows various students and the distance they travel everyday to get to Yoobee. Most students tend to be travelling under 15km per day to get to Yoobee, with a few outliers that seem to make their daily from atleast 30km away, with the highest travel journey on this data set being 92.1km from the Horowhenua district.

Student Authenticity on Various Catergories


This Bubble Chart represents the various differences amoung students at Yoobee. With a centralised age range between 17 to 25. This data set shows the diversity among students in regards to personal preferences when it comes to favourite animal, suburb they live in, distance they travel and their age. This data represents the creative demeanor that Yoobee has as a Design school with a collection of personal preferences that are relayed in a creative form, that are visualised in the classroom.
